Garage Door Opener Overview

  • What is a Garage Door Opener? A garage door opener is a motorized device that opens and closes a garage door. The name, however, is a bit misleading. Garage Door Openers are only meant to pull about 20 pounds, while your garage door weighs anywhere between 120lbs to 400lbs. In truth, the spring system is really what opens your door. The garage door opener assists, but doesn't opener your door. This is important because your issue might be the door itself. You might have alignment issues, tracks the are too right, or a spring that doesn't have the right amount of tension
  • Types of Garage Door Openers. Three main garage door openers exist: 1. Belt Drives 2. Chain Drives 3. Screw (aka Worm) Drives. Belt Drives are the best option of the three and most popular and screw drives have largely been phased out. Chain drives have been around forever but are inferior to belt drives. Belt drives are better for three reasons: 1. They last longer 2. Require less maintenance and 3. have better warranties

Repair or Replace

  • This depends on the underlying issue. If it a simple fix or adjustment like a sensor needs to be replaced, then repair is the best. But if you have bigger issues like a bad logic board or the gears are stripped out, then replacement is the best. In our experience, it is usually better to replace. You are looking at an all in cost of $450+ and have the piece of mind that you won't have to pay for the same issue 2 or 3 times.
  • How often do you use your garage door opener? If it is like your front door, then it make more sense to replace. If you have detached garage where you store stuff, then it isn't as important.
  • How old is your opener? The expected life of a garage door opener is 8 years. If yours is older, you got your monies worth. Anything over 10 years, it isn't worth putting any money into it

Advanced Features

  • Wifi is the biggest upgrade to garage door openers in the past decade. Virtually all models have it. Allows you to open your garage door from your phone. Super convenient for getting things dropped off, without giving access to your home itself.
  • Amazon Key is a great amenity if you get packages delivered and you want to know they've been safely delivered. Amazon can access your garage
  • Smart garage doors mean different different folks. For some, it means having WiFi. For others, it means a system that will integrate with Alexa or some other platform-logo
  • In Houston, we can count on two things. 1. The power goes out and 2. Someone is going to try and rob you. Upgraded features include battery backup, built in bright lighting, and cameras
