Hollow Metal Doors

Professional-grade security and durability for your commercial facility

Introduction to Hollow Metal Doors

Choosing a commercial metal door can be tough, with many factors to consider. Generally, it should be secure, reliable, and built to last.

One of the most popular for industrial and public facilities is the hollow metal door. You've probably seen them before in hospitals, office buildings, warehouses, or commercial entrances, for example. These doors are strong and durable enough to withstand physical and fire attacks. Yet, that's not all.

Keep reading to find out the full potential of hollow metal doors and how they can improve the security, durability, and functionality of your facility.

Purposes of a Hollow Metal Door

A hollow metal door is one of the most popular choices for commercial use. They come in varying sizes and are made of stainless steel. Metal sheets and insulation materials like honeycomb or polystyrene are combined. You can choose a single door or double door model.

Best applications for hollow metal doors? These doors can be installed in all kinds of industrial buildings, both exterior and interior. You can find them in:

  • Hotels
  • Schools
  • Conference centers
  • Warehouses
  • Administrative buildings
  • Hospitals
  • Manufacturing facilities

Advantages of Hollow Metal Doors

We've already defined what a hollow metal door is and where you can find it. Now, let's discuss why these doors are so popular among commercial building owners and what benefits they offer:

Low Maintenance

These doors don't require a lot of upkeep. Made from galvanized stainless steel, they resist various outside conditions and are not prone to corrosion.

Durability

Their biggest advantage is their longevity. Hollow metal doors last much longer than regular wooden or plastic doors and resist damage and wear.
